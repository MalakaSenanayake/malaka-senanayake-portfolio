About Me

Hello! I'm Malaka Senanayake

I’m a results-oriented Senior Software Engineer with over 7 years of experience in building scalable, high-performance enterprise applications. With a BEng in Software Engineering, I specialize in Java (Java 17), Spring Boot, Angular, and AWS, focusing on micro-services, API design, and cloud-native solutions.


Currently, I lead the full design and implementation of an Invoice Management System for Kevilton Distributor—driving automation, scalability, and efficiency across operations. Before this, I contributed to Modjoul’s IoT-driven safety platform at Virtusa, where I integrated AWS IoT, Lambda, and CI/CD pipelines to enhance real-time data processing and cloud performance.


I’m experienced in Agile and DevOps environments, performance tuning, and mentoring cross-functional teams. Known for problem-solving and adaptability, I’m passionate about continuous learning, system optimization, and taking on complex technical challenges.


Outside of work, I find peace in solo drives along the seaside and through forest trails — it’s my way of reconnecting with nature and clearing my mind. During my free time, I enjoy creating pencil art, capturing quiet moments and emotions through sketches. Mindfulness is also a big part of my life; I often spend time meditating in forest monasteries, which helps me recharge and stay balanced amid the busyness of everyday life. I also have a deep appreciation for farming — nurturing plants and watching them grow reminds me of the value of patience and consistency.
